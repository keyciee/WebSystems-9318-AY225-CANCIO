About the College

Our Mission

The College of Computer Studies is dedicated to providing world-class education in computing and information technology. We strive to develop competent, innovative, and ethical professionals who can contribute to the advancement of technology and society.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution in computer science education, recognized for excellence in teaching, research, and community engagement, producing graduates who are globally competitive and socially responsible.

Core Values

Excellence

Pursuing the highest standards in education and research

Innovation

Fostering creativity and technological advancement

Integrity

Upholding ethical principles in all endeavors

Collaboration

Working together to achieve common goals

History

Founded in 1995, the College of Computer Studies has grown from a small department to a comprehensive institution offering multiple undergraduate and graduate programs. Our state-of-the-art facilities and dedicated faculty have helped thousands of students launch successful careers in technology.

Accreditations

  • Accredited by ABET (Accreditation Board for Engineering and Technology)
  • ISO 9001:2015 Certified
  • Member of ACM (Association for Computing Machinery)
  • Partner institution with major tech companies
